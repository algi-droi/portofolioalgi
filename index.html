About Me

I am Audi Alghifari, a graphic designer and photographer with a strong passion for creating visuals that are impactful, aesthetic, and meaningful. For me, design and photography are not just about appearance, but about how to convey stories, emotions, and identity through every piece of work. In design, I focus on detailed visual execution, from composition and color to creative concepts that help build the character of a brand or project. In photography, I am used to capturing moments with a natural approach while maintaining strong artistic value. I continuously develop my skills by exploring various visual styles and keeping up with trends, ensuring that every work I create remains relevant, fresh, and has its own distinctive identity
